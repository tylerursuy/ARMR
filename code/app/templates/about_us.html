ARMR - Audio Recorded Medical Record



"I went into medicine to work with people, not be a data clerk."

With ARMR you can...


  • Benefit the physician and patient interaction
  • Free up to 20-45% of a physician’s time
  • Integrate with existing EMR systems
  • Increase hospital revenue
